Kavak ranks 2022-2023 semi-used vehicles that offer the best value for money in Mexico, with savings reaching 57% compared to dealership sticker prices. Features like Apple CarPlay and Android Auto appear in roughly 80-90% of 2022-2023 models, with minimal advancement in newer years, making the price difference rather than capability the key factor. The Dodge Journey 2022, MG HS 2022, and Volvo XC40 2022 top Kavak's ranking for strongest depreciation curves, offering savings exceeding 55% off manufacturer suggested retail prices.
