The article explains how a larger down payment (enganche) reduces both your monthly car payment and the total cost of credit. With Kavak Crédito the minimum down payment is 15% of the car's value, five points higher than traditional banking's floor for used cars, and the range that best balances monthly payment and liquidity is between 20% and 30%.
